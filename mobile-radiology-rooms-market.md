<p><p>The mobile radiology rooms market is categorized into two primary types: pull-type and self-propelled units. Pull-type mobile radiology rooms are towed by vehicles, offering flexibility in transport while requiring a separate power source. In contrast, self-propelled mobile units are equipped with their own propulsion systems, allowing for independent movement and quicker deployment. Both types enhance access to radiological services, especially in remote areas or during emergencies, facilitating timely diagnosis and patient care.</p></p>
