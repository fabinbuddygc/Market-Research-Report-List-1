Metal film incorporates thin layers of metal particles for superior heat and UV protection, contributing to energy efficiency. Sputtering film, created through a vacuum deposition process, delivers high-quality clarity and durability, ensuring effective heat rejection and safety. These films enhance vehicle comfort and protection, appealing to consumers seeking both style and functionality.</p></p>
