<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Rivet Squeezer</li><li>Hydraulic Rivet Squeezer</li><li>Electric Rivet Squeezer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rivet squeezer market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic rivet squeezers utilize compressed air to apply force, making them favored in high-volume applications. Hydraulic rivet squeezers harness fluid pressure for powerful, controlled squeezing, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Electric rivet squeezers operate on electricity, offering precision and ease of use, often preferred in smaller workshops or assembly lines. Each type caters to specific needs in rivet fastening, balancing power, speed, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
