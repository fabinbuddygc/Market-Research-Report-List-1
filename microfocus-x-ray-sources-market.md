<p><strong>The Microfocus X-ray Sources Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-tube</li><li>Sealed-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microfocus X-ray sources are categorized into open-tube and sealed-tube types, each serving distinct applications. Open-tube sources utilize a vacuum environment and allow users to adjust the target material, offering flexibility and higher performance for specific needs. In contrast, sealed-tube sources come pre-filled with gas and are designed for lower maintenance and longer operational life, making them ideal for portable or industrial applications. Both types provide precise imaging capabilities but differ in versatility and longevity.</p></p>
