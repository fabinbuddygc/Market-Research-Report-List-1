<p><ul><li>Pure Polyurea</li><li>Half Polyurea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurea market is primarily divided into two types: pure polyurea and half polyurea. Pure polyurea, characterized by its rapid curing and superior flexibility, is ideal for applications requiring strong resistance to chemicals and abrasion, making it suitable for coatings and sealants. Half polyurea, on the other hand, contains a mixture of polyurea and other polymers, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. This type is often used in applications where some flexibility is needed, but high-performance characteristics are not critical.</p></p>
