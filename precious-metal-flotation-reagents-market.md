<p><ul><li>Collector</li><li>Activator</li><li>Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precious metal flotation reagents market includes three main types: collectors, activators, and inhibitors. Collectors are chemicals that enhance the attachment of ore particles to air bubbles, aiding in mineral separation. Activators boost the effectiveness of collectors by promoting the attachment of specific minerals during the flotation process. Inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent certain unwanted minerals from attaching to bubbles, allowing for a purer concentrate. Together, these reagents play a crucial role in optimizing the extraction of precious metals.</p></p>
