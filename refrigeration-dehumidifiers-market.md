<p><p>Refrigeration dehumidifiers are categorized into three main types: Normal Type, Heat Pipe Type, and New Air Type. </p><p>Normal Type uses a compressor and evaporator to cool air, condensing moisture to lower humidity. Heat Pipe Type enhances efficiency by utilizing a heat exchanger, improving moisture removal without significant energy use. New Air Type introduces fresh air while dehumidifying, making it effective for spaces needing improved air quality. Each type serves specific applications based on efficiency, energy consumption, and air quality requirements.</p></p>
