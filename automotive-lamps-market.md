<p><p>The automotive lamps market encompasses various lighting technologies, including halogen, HID, and LED lighting. Halogen lighting is a traditional and cost-effective option, providing bright illumination but with shorter lifespans. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lighting offers greater brightness and energy efficiency, often seen in luxury vehicles, but requires a warm-up time. LED lighting is rapidly gaining popularity due to its long lifespan, energy efficiency, and versatility in design, making it a preferred choice for modern vehicles and enhancing overall safety.</p></p>
