<p><p>The Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) panels and sheets market encompasses various types based on the reinforcing fibers used. Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) offers excellent corrosion resistance and is widely used in construction and marine sectors. Boron Fiber Reinforced Polymer (BFRP) provides superior thermal stability, while Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is valued for its impact resistance, commonly utilized in military and protective gear.</p></p>
