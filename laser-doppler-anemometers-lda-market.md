<p><ul><li>Forward Scatter Receiver</li><li>Backward Scatter Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Doppler Anemometers (LDA) are advanced flow measurement devices that utilize the Doppler effect to determine the velocity of particles within a fluid. The market for LDAs is categorized into two main types: Forward Scatter Receiver and Backward Scatter Receiver. Forward Scatter Receivers capture light scattered in the direction of the laser beam, providing high spatial resolution, while Backward Scatter Receivers detect light scattered back towards the source, offering enhanced sensitivity and the ability to measure larger particles or flows at greater distances.</p></p>
