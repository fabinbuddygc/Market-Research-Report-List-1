<p><p>Lithium battery protection boards are essential for ensuring the safety and longevity of lithium-ion batteries. The market is divided into two main types: hard boards and soft boards. Hard boards are rigid, often made from materials like fiberglass, providing robust protection against physical damage and heat. In contrast, soft boards are flexible, allowing for versatile applications and easier integration in compact designs. Both types feature essential circuitry to prevent overcharging, over-discharging, and short circuits, enhancing battery performance and safety.</p></p>
