<p><ul><li>Foley Catheters</li><li>Intermittent Catheters</li><li>External Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable urinary catheter market is divided into three main types: Foley catheters, intermittent catheters, and external catheters. Foley catheters are indwelling devices designed for long-term use, typically secured with a balloon. Intermittent catheters are used for short-term drainage and can be inserted as needed, promoting patient independence. External catheters, also known as condom catheters, are external devices applied to the penis, offering a non-invasive alternative for male patients. These types cater to diverse patient needs and preferences in urinary management.</p></p>
