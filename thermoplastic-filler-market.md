<p><p>The thermoplastic filler market encompasses various types, including oxides, silicates, hydroxides, and metals. Oxides, such as titanium dioxide, enhance opacity and durability. Silicates, including talc and mica, improve mechanical strength and thermal stability. Hydroxides, like aluminum hydroxide, provide flame retardancy and improve viscosity. Metals, such as aluminum and copper, enhance conductivity and strength. These fillers are utilized to modify the properties of thermoplastic materials, enhancing performance and expanding applications across industries like automotive, packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
