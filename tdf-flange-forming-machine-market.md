<p><p>TDF Flange Forming Machines are essential in manufacturing processes that require forming flanges on sheet metal. The market is divided into two main types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex machines perform flange forming in a single pass, making them ideal for lower volume production with straightforward designs. In contrast, Duplex machines offer dual functionality, allowing for simultaneous operations, which significantly increases productivity and is suited for high-volume applications. Both types cater to varying industry needs, balancing efficiency and complexity.</p></p>
