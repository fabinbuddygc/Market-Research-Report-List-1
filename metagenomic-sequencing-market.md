<p><p>The metagenomic sequencing market encompasses various approaches, including shotgun metagenomic sequencing, which analyzes total genomic DNA from environmental samples; 16S rRNA sequencing, focusing on bacterial taxonomic identification; and metatranscriptomics, which examines RNA to understand gene expression profiles. Whole-genome sequencing captures the complete DNA sequences of organisms in a sample, while de novo assembly reconstructs genomes from sequencing data without a reference. These techniques provide insights into microbial diversity, function, and ecological interactions.</p></p>
