<p><ul><li>Carbotherm al reduction method</li><li>Self-propagating Reaction（SHS）</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Diboride market primarily consists of two production methods: Carbothermal reduction and Self-propagating High-temperature Synthesis (SHS). </p><p>The Carbothermal reduction method involves reacting titanium dioxide and boron carbide at high temperatures to produce titanium diboride, emphasizing efficiency and purity. In contrast, the SHS method relies on exothermic reactions between titanium and boron powders, generating high temperatures that instantly synthesize titanium diboride, making it a cost-effective and rapid approach. Both methods cater to various applications, including aerospace, defense, and cutting tools.</p></p>
