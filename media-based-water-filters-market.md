<p><p>The media-based water filters market comprises several types, primarily including reverse osmosis (RO) systems and activated carbon filters. RO-based filters utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants, providing highly purified water suitable for domestic and industrial use. Activated carbon filters, on the other hand, use carbon to adsorb impurities and improve taste and odor. Other types may include UV filters and sediment filters, each catering to specific filtration needs and offering varying levels of water purification efficiency.</p></p>
