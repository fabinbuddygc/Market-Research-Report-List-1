Centrifuges separate components based on density, crucial for purification. Dryers remove moisture from materials, enhancing shelf life and stability. Granulators consolidate powders into granules for better handling. Other equipment includes specialized tools tailored to specific processing needs in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
