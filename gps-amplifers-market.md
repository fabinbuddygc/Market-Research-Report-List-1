<p><ul><li>Mono Amplifiers</li><li>Multichannel Amplifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS amplifiers market is categorized into mono amplifiers and multichannel amplifiers. Mono amplifiers enhance a single signal channel, providing focused amplification for specific applications, often in smaller or simpler setups. In contrast, multichannel amplifiers support multiple signals simultaneously, ideal for complex systems requiring diverse frequency management, such as in sophisticated navigation systems. The choice between these types depends on the application's demands, with mono amplifiers favoring simplicity and multichannel amplifiers offering versatility and broader capabilities.</p></p>
