<p><p>The fire protection valves market consists of various types, each serving specific functions. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional fluid movement. Butterfly valves provide quick shut-off and are lightweight, ideal for large pipelines. Gate valves offer minimal pressure loss, making them suitable for isolation. Ball valves ensure tight sealing and durability, allowing for easy operation. Other valves include globe and diaphragm valves, which cater to specialized applications. Together, these valves are essential for maintaining safety and efficiency in fire protection systems.</p></p>
