<p><p>The Gantry Industrial Robot market comprises various types based on the number of axes they operate on. 1-Axis robots are designed for linear movements, primarily along a single direction. 2-Axis robots offer enhanced flexibility with two degrees of freedom, enabling simple planar movements. 3-Axis robots add vertical movement capabilities, ideal for pick and place tasks. 4-Axis robots introduce rotational capabilities for more complex applications. The 'Others' category includes specialized designs like delta robots or articulated variants, catering to unique operational needs.</p></p>
