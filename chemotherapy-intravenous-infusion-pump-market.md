<p><p>The chemotherapy intravenous infusion pump market consists of three main types: large volume pumps, syringe pumps, and elastomeric pumps. Large volume pumps are designed to deliver a substantial amount of fluid over an extended period, ideal for prolonged therapies. Syringe pumps provide precise control for administering smaller volumes of medication, aiding in accurate dosing. Elastomeric pumps are portable and utilize a flexible reservoir to deliver medications continuously, offering convenience and mobility for outpatient treatments. Each type enhances patient care and treatment efficacy.</p></p>
