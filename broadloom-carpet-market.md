<p><p>Broadloom carpet market consists of three main types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile carpets have individual yarn tufts that are sheared, providing a soft and luxurious feel, ideal for residential spaces. Loop pile features yarns that are looped, offering durability and texture, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Cut and loop pile combines both types, creating unique patterns and visual interest while balancing comfort and resilience, catering to diverse aesthetic preferences and functional needs.</p></p>
