<p><p>The Computerized Physician Order Entry (CPOE) market can be categorized into two main types: standalone and integrated. Standalone CPOE systems function independently, allowing healthcare providers to manage orders without being connected to other hospital systems. In contrast, integrated CPOE systems are part of a larger electronic health record (EHR) platform, facilitating seamless communication across various departments and improving efficiency. Both types aim to enhance patient safety and streamline order management, but they differ in their scope and connectivity.</p></p>
