<p><p>The bike racks and carriers market encompasses various types designed for different vehicle setups and user preferences. Rear mounted bike racks attach to the back of vehicles, offering easy access and installation. Roof mounted bike racks secure bikes on the vehicle's roof, providing a streamlined profile and keeping the rear accessible, but requiring lifting. Towbar mounted bike racks connect to the vehicle's tow bar, allowing for heavy payloads and convenient access while maintaining stability during transit. Each type caters to specific user needs.</p></p>
