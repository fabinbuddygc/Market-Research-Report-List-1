<p><p>The electrical wireway market is primarily categorized into two types: metal wireway and non-metallic wireway. Metal wireways, typically made from steel or aluminum, provide robust protection and durability, making them suitable for industrial and commercial applications. They offer advantages like superior grounding and heat dissipation. Non-metallic wireways, often constructed from PVC or fiberglass, are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, making them ideal for residential use or environments where moisture is a concern. Both types cater to specific installation needs and environments.</p></p>
