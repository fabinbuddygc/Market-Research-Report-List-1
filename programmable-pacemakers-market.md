<p><strong>The Programmable Pacemakers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Pacemakers</li><li>Dual Chamber Pacemakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable pacemakers market features two primary types: single chamber and dual chamber pacemakers. Single chamber pacemakers are designed to manage either the atrium or the ventricle, providing simpler pacing solutions typically for specific arrhythmias. In contrast, dual chamber pacemakers stimulate both the atrium and ventricle, promoting coordinated heartbeats and better overall heart function. The growth of these market segments is driven by advancements in technology, increasing prevalence of heart diseases, and a rising aging population requiring cardiac care.</p></p>
