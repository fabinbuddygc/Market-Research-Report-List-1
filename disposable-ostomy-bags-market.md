<p><p>The disposable ostomy bags market primarily consists of two types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags integrate the adhesive skin barrier and the pouch into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, two-piece bags feature a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for flexibility in changing the pouch without removing the barrier, enhancing comfort and convenience for users. Both types cater to varying user preferences and medical needs, driving growth in the ostomy care market.</p></p>
