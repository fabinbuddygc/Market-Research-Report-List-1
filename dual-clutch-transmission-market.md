<p><p>The dual-clutch transmission (DCT) market is primarily segmented into dry clutch and wet clutch types. Dry clutch systems use no fluid, allowing for lighter weight and lower drag, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. Conversely, wet clutch systems operate submerged in transmission fluid, providing better cooling and smoother operation under high torque conditions. These distinctions make dry clutches suitable for sporty performance vehicles, while wet clutches are often favored in heavy-duty applications or for enhanced durability in various driving conditions.</p></p>
