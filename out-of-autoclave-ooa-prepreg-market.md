<p><p>The Out-of-Autoclave (OOA) prepreg market includes two primary types: unidirectional and directional prepregs. Unidirectional prepregs consist of continuous fiber aligned in a single direction, offering high strength and stiffness for applications requiring reinforcement in specific orientations. In contrast, directional prepregs feature fibers arranged in multiple directions, providing enhanced adaptability and processing flexibility. Both types are utilized in industries such as aerospace and automotive, where performance and weight reduction are critical.</p></p>
